Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam

Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in India. Millions of devotees visit the temple every year to seek blessings from Lord Venkateswara.

The temple, located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, is a major attraction for pilgrims and tourists alike. Known for its spiritual significance and architectural beauty, the temple attracts devotees from all over the world.
